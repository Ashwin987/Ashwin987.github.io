Predicting Student GPA Using Lifestyle Factors

Tech: R, Multinomial Logistic Regression, Statistical Modeling

Analyzed the influence of student lifestyle factors (sleep, study habits, social hours) on academic performance. Performed extensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) including log-transformations and variable recoding to correct for skewness. Built and compared four Multinomial Logistic Regression models to classify GPA into Low, Average, and High tiers. Validated model performance using 10-fold Cross-Validation, Confusion Matrices, and ROC/AUC analysis to identify key predictors like study time efficiency versus social distractions.
