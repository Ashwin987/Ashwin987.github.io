AFC Bournemouth Scouting Report - Summer 2025
R • Clustering • Radar Charts • Efficiency Scoring

Developed a data-driven recruitment strategy to support AFC Bournemouth's summer transfer window, targeting replacements for key departures under Andoni Iraola's high-pressing system. Built a rigorous data pipeline using R to scrape and normalize statistics from FBref and Transfermarkt, adjusting for league difficulty.

Utilized K-Means clustering and custom Efficiency Scores to identify players fitting the tactical profile—specifically high-line goalkeepers and ball-playing defenders. The analysis successfully identified Bafodé Diakité as a top target; the model was validated when the club actually signed him, marking a major success for this scouting framework.

Monitoring Injury Risk & Performance: Chelsea FC
Python • Power BI • Random Forest • Time-Series

Addressed the challenge of balancing match demands and recovery for Chelsea FC. Developed the Load Balance Efficiency Score (LBES), a composite metric (0-100) that unifies GPS, wellness, and physical testing data to quantify player readiness.

Implemented a Random Forest Classifier to predict injury risk sessions with 100% precision for high-risk cases. Designed an interactive Power BI dashboard for performance staff to visualize trends, detecting "red zone" spikes in workload (>20% variance) and estimating recovery timelines.

Optimizing FPL Scoring for Defensive Midfielders
Python • Web Scraping • FBref Data

Built a web scraper to extract player data from the FPL archive and match reports from FBref to analyze the undervaluation of defensive midfielders. Proposed and tested a new scoring metric: awarding 1 point for every 3 ball recoveries.

This adjustment closes the point gap between defensive midfielders and attacking players, making the game more strategically diverse without overcomplicating the rules.
