EPL Team Performance Analysis Using Modeling & Visualization

Tech: R (sqldf, glm), Poisson Regression, K-Means Clustering, Tableau

Investigated the efficiency of English Premier League clubs by analyzing the relationship between financial resources and on-field success. I collected data from FBref and Transfermarkt, using R's sqldf package to merge seasonal performance metrics with squad characteristics.

I applied K-Means Clustering to identify "efficient buyers" (like Brighton & Brentford) and implemented Poisson Regression models to predict win totals. The analysis revealed that while relative spending is a significant predictor, older squads and high player turnover rates show a statistically significant negative impact on performance.
